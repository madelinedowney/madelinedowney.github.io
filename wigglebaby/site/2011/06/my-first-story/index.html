My First Story

June 21, 2011 at 10:04 pm · Filed under Rants

Ghostwriter’s note: I’ve had a hard time deciding what to do with this site now that Maddie is old enough to speak for herself. Turns out, I’m out of a job! What follows is her first written story, as transcribed by her mom. —JD

Context

“Sodo” is Maddie’s favorite fabricated word. This story was told while playing with a new stamp set that features emotional faces.

The Story

There was an angry stamp.

And the others were not sad.
That one is happy.

They go to their house and they have people there.
Then they sleep in the bedrooms.
This one’s bedroom and his bedroom and his bedroom…

[she mumbles something about the Moon and tries to draw one. I wrote the word "MOON" and she wrote it too! —KK]

When they sleep, when they heard a noise. A bird.
They climbed on it.
When they heard a noise, this was another tweet.
When they go on a Sodo, that’s the end!!

Now they heard another noise.
When they climbed on it, they begin to cry.
And Maddie’s here. And said Maddie be careful with the stamps!
They get on the bird when they heard a noise.
They say, “I wanna go home.”
Then they heard another noise, then they can go on their Sodo.
They begin to cry.

The Sodo is yellow.
When they heard a noise, like this [scribbles her marker really fast].
Froggy goes to the doctor with his mommy and sister and daddy.
Doctor’s name is Adios.
When they begin to cry a little, poor guys!!!
They don’t have to be sleepy – they just have to tell mommy’s and daddo’s.
When they go on a Sodo, they leave.
They go home.
They make some dinner.
